Kurji Tat restaurant

Expanding the beloved food spot to the internet.

Kurji Tat needed a quick and reliable solution to modernise their web presence.

Their customers have been given an improved online experience, since they can find their lunch easily. Their overall reputation is now encased in a more modern design.
